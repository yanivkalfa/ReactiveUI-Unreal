### 6b. Factual errors on existing pages (verified against source; several are in the pages
authored 2026-07-14 ‚Äî recorded here with the same severity as anything else)

   ‚Äî every demo does it and the formatter corpus pins it ("exploded returns",
   `uetkx-formatter-cases.json`). Both samples would not compile.
